Welcome to demand-supply visualizer of electricity

This system is an visualization tool for demand and supply data of the electricity in India.

System has been designed for the energy planner, state board of electricity and various electricity generation companies.

By this they can predict their potential customer (in case of surplus state) and provider (in case of deficit state) in terms of electricity.
